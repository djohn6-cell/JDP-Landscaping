# JDP Landscaping Services — Charlotte, NC Area

JDP Landscaping offers full-service landscaping for residential and commercial properties throughout the greater Charlotte, NC area.

## Services

### Mulching

Fresh dark mulch applied around plants and beds. Protects roots, retains moisture, reduces weeds, and gives every bed a clean, finished look. Most properties benefit from a fresh application once or twice a year.

### Trimming

Precise shrub and hedge trimming that keeps growth controlled and your property looking sharp. Regular trimming promotes healthy plant growth and maintains a well-maintained appearance.

### Removal

Shrubs, trees, overgrowth, and unwanted vegetation removed cleanly and hauled away. Includes overgrown hedges, dead trees, invasive plants, and post-storm debris.

### Planting

Plant selection and installation suited to your soil, sunlight, and space. The right plants in the right places — chosen for long-term health and appearance.

### Yard Cleanup

Seasonal and post-storm cleanups. Debris cleared, beds tidied, property refreshed. Handles everything from routine end-of-season cleanup to storm damage recovery.

## Service Area
